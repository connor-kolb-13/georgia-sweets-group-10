Meet the Owner!

Thank you for checking out my website! Georgia Sweets is a home bakery located in Oak Creek, WI that specializes in decorated cookies and other sweet treats. I am a proud mom of a little boy and girl and law enforcement officer wife. Each treat I make is so special to me and I’d love to make your next event extra “sweet”!


I launched Georgia Sweets in September of 2022. I was on maternity leave, and I had extra time to create the sweets for my son’s second birthday. Following the party, I had so many requests for a variety of treats that I decided to start a business! I feel honored each time I am asked to create a treat for an event, so I ensure that the detail in every treat is exactly what you want!
