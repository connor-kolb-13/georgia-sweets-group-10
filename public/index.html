Hello!

Welcome to Georgia Sweets, a home bakery located in Oak Creek, WI, where every treat is baked with love and attention to detail. Our owner, a proud mom of two and a dedicated law enforcement officer wife, pours her heart and soul into every sweet treat that she creates. From her specialty decorated cookies to other sweet treats, she strives to make your special occasion extra "sweet"!


Georgia Sweets was launched in September of 2022 during the owner's maternity leave. She had extra time to create the perfect treats for her son's second birthday party and following the party, she received many requests for her delicious baked goods. She decided to turn her passion into a business and hasn't looked back since. Each treat she creates is so special to her, and she feels honored every time she is asked to create something for an event.


At Georgia Sweets, we believe that every event deserves a special touch. That's why our owner ensures that the detail in every treat is exactly what you want! Whether it's a birthday party, wedding, or any other special occasion, our bakery is here to provide you with the best baked goods around. We look forward to making your next event extra "sweet" with our delectable treats!
